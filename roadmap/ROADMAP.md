# Learn to Claude — The Full Journey

One hundred challenges, ten series. From your first conversation with an
AI agent to shipping real software — each challenge a short lesson plus a
practical task using your own work, each series ending with a project you
design yourself.

**Series 1 (Foundations) is free and included in this plugin.**
Series 2–10 are paid challenge packs: **€20 per series** (10 challenges)
or **€100 for everything** (all 90 remaining challenges). Packs are
delivered through the companion — same format, same coach, deeper water.

Where you'll work: Series 1–4 live entirely in Cowork. Series 5–6 add
GitHub and your first public website. Series 7 introduces Claude Code.
Series 8–10 combine everything.

---
